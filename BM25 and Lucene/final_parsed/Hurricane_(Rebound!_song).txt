hurricane  rebound  song   wikipedia hurricane is an english-language swedish hit debut single by the swedish boy band duo rebound made up of rabih jaber and eddie razaz the song was written by svante halldin jakob hazell and teddy sky and was produced by svante halldin and jakob hazellthe electro-pop song hurricane was released on 12 april 2010 and reached the top of the swedish singles chart on the chart dated 7 may 2010
