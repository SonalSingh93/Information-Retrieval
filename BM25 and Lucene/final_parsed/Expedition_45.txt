expedition 45  wikipedia expedition 45 was the 45th expedition to the international space station scott kelly and mikhail korniyenko transferred from expedition 44 as part of their year-long stay aboard the iss expedition 45 began with the arrival of soyuz tma-18m at the iss on september 11 2015 and concluded with the departure of soyuz tma-17m on december 11 2015 kelly korniyenko and sergey volkov then transferred to the crew of expedition 46
