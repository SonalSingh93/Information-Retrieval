springfield  west virginia  wikipedia springfield is a census-designated place cdp in northwestern hampshire county in the us state of west virginia as of the 2010 census springfield had a population of 477 springfield is located north of romney along west virginia route 28 at its junction with green spring road west virginia secondary route 1 and springfield pike west virginia secondary route 3 it is currently considering incorporationestablished on december 16,1790 at the cross roads of hampshire county on the property of william and samuel abernethy by an act of the virginia general assembly springfield was named in commemoration of the battle of springfield 1780george washington first visited the springfield area in 1748 as a member of a party that surveyed the land holdings of thomas fairfax 6th lord fairfax of cameron in the south branch potomac river valleyin june 1861 the town organized a company of men known as the potomac guards in support of the confederacy the company was under the command of captain philip t graceon august 23 1861 springfield played host to an american civil war skirmish between the unionists and the confederatestoday springfield is the site of a number of historic private residences dating from the 18th and 19th centuries media related to springfield west virginia at wikimedia commons
