expedition 32  wikipedia expedition 32 was the 32nd long-duration expedition to the international space station iss it began on 1 july 2012 with the departure from the iss of the soyuz tma-03m spacecraft which returned the expedition 31 crew to earth and concluded on 16 september 2012 with the departure of soyuz tma-04m the soyuz craft returned to earth on 17 september 2012 at 6:53am moscow standard time when touchdown was officially recorded by the russian federal space agency
